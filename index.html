SSD Storage

A solid-state drive (SSD) is a new generation of storage device used in computers. SSDs use flash-based memory, which is much faster than a traditional mechanical hard disk. Free Content Delivery Network (CDN)

CloudFlare, an advanced CDN, is built directly into your account and it automatically increases the performance of your site. Enable CloudFlare instantly, for free, on any of your websites without any coding changes.

One-Click Apps

The One Click Installer allows you to install a range of popular applications, such as WordPress, Joomla!, Drupal or Coppermine to your web server with ease. The service is available from within your Fasthosts control panel.
